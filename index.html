User Guide 📖

  • Select an instrument by clicking its name.
  • Click on the pads to activate or deactivate steps for that instrument.
  • For Bass, adjust the pitch of each step using the knob that appears on active pads.
  • Use the mute button (🔇), solo button (🔊), and the volume sliders next to each instrument to control sound levels.
  • Adjust the tempo using the slider under 'Tempo'.
  • Adjust the swing using the slider under 'Swing'.
  • Adjust the master volume using the 'Master Volume' slider at the top right.
  • Adjust the Low-Pass Filter cutoff frequency using the 'Low-Pass Filter' slider.
  • Adjust the High-Pass Filter cutoff frequency using the 'High-Pass Filter' slider.
  • Use the Equalizer controls to adjust Low, Mid, and High frequencies.
  • Use the Bass EQ controls to adjust the Bass instrument's Low, Mid, and High frequencies.
  • Use the ADSR controls to shape the sound of the Kick and Hi-Hat. Click on 'ADSR Controls ▼' to expand or collapse the menu.
  • Click 'Play' ▶️ to start the sequence and 'Stop' ⏹️ to stop it.
  • You can use 'Random Bass Line' 🎲 to generate a 16-step random bass sequence duplicated to fill 32 steps.
